Salon du Chocolat and Deliveroo: a match made in chocolate heaven

  • 18 February 2019
  • Jorina
  • Read time: 3 min
salon du chocolat millésime chocolat van dender frederic bloondeel benoit nihant chocolatiers pralines chocolate boxes chocolate
Share this article

Chocolate lovers unite! From 22 until 24 February, the Salon du Chocolat will take place at Tour & Taxis in Brussels again and you can be part of it! Together with the Salon du Chocolat, we are not only giving away  seven duo tickets to attend the Salon but also delivering chocolates from four top Belgian chocolatiers in Brussels, Liège, Antwerp and Waterloo. Read to find out more.

Millésime Chocolat

Located in Liège, Millésime Chocolat is one of the few bean-to-bar workshops in Belgium that works directly with cocoa beans. Each chocolate comes from a specific region or country giving it a unique and unexpected flavour. Treat yourself with their box Dégustation and discover six delicious chocolate bars from six different regions.

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Mélanie Notari (@melanienotari) on Dec 18, 2018 at 6:47am PST

Benoit Nihant

For most, chocolate is a delicious treat to enjoy alone, to give or to share but for Benoit Nihant it's more than a passion, it's a work of art. Using machines, some more than 200 years old, he recreates original flavours that are all but forgotten. Enjoy their Haute Couture Prélude box with 20 chocolates and discover a world of delights.

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Benoit Nihant Chocolatier (@benoitnihant) on Oct 2, 2017 at 4:16am PDT

 

Frederic Bloondeel

Frederic Blondeel is more than a chocolatier, he's also a passionate roaster. From bean to bar, from selecting the right cocoa beans to using a traditional, historic roaster, all lead to the right end product: intense chocolate bars, elegant pralines and so much more. Why not try their pralines box with different ganaches, caramels, milk, dark and white selection and hearts? Trust us, you won't be disappointed.

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Frederic Blondeel Official (@fredericblondeel) on Apr 24, 2018 at 11:57pm PDT

Van Dender

Being the official supplier to the Belgian Court since 2008, Herman Van Dender has gathered a lot of fame and prestige as a pastry chef. For him making chocolate is art, that's why it took two years to finalize their chocolate receipts and create pralines with unique flavours. A must try!

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Van Dender Chocolates (@vandenderchocolates) on Feb 1, 2018 at 1:08am PST

Salon du Chocolat

With over 130 participants, 8,000 sqm dedicated to chocolate and cocoa, the Salon du Chocolat promises to be one to remember. On the programme: a Chocolate Fashion Show, workshops, conferences, demonstrations, chocolate art exhibitions and so much more. The event chocolate lovers cannot miss. To win one of our seven duo tickets, visit our facebook page, answer the question and tag your friend*. It's that easy!

Chocolate is the answer no matter what the question is ;). Order your chocolate box here.
