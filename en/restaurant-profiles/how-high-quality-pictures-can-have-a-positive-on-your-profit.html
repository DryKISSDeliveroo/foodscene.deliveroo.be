Once you have attracted a customer to your Deliveroo page, the next step is to convince him or her with the visualization of the dish. Just like the employees in your restaurant, pictures help customers to make a choice easier.

What about menu pictures?

By using a picture for every menu item, you make it easy for customers to make their decision. Not only does it give the client an idea of what he or she is about the order, but it also gives them a 'virtual' taste of your restaurant food. Overall your Deliveroo page will have more character and the quality of the pictures will enhance the customer's experience.
