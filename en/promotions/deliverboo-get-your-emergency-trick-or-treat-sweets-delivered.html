Deliver-Boo! Get your emergency trick or treat sweets delivered.

  • 26 October 2018
  • Zaïna
  • Read time: 2 min
deliveroo halloween trick sweets
Share this article

What's more terrifying than Halloween? Not having any sweets to give out to the hangry trick or treaters at your door. But never fear, because you can order emergency trick or treat sweets from Deliveroo in Belgium. We know what it's like when you forget to pick up your Halloween sweets – just sitting in the dark and pretending you're not in every time the doorbell goes. But no one wants to disappoint the little witches and wizards waiting outside.

All you have to do is open the Deliveroo app, click on the Halloween Emergency Candy Shop and in no time, there will be a friendly knock on the door. With this solution, you will be fully stocked with enough sugar for the hocus pocus season!

Do it quick, you have only until 31st October Deliver-Boo.
