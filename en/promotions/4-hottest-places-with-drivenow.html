Drive in style to the hottest places in Brussels  

  • 18 May 2017
  • Laura
  • Read time: 4 min
brussels hottest places partnership ixelles city center
Share this article

An important Tinder date coming up? Need to impress your foodie friends? Here are 4 of the hottest restaurants in Brussels where you can also order from the comfort of your home. But you're feeling more adventurous? What about exploring the city and going there in style with a brand new Mini or BMW? How cool would that be?! We partnered up with our friends at DriveNow to show you the best places to be right now! They even have a little gift for you… See at the end of the article.  

For the perfect date

Need to show your date that you know all about the best addresses and that you love to travel? Take your tinder match to Bao Bang Bang in a Mini Cabrio. A special bun filled with deliciousness coming straight from Taïwan. A must try!

Where: Bao Bang Bang in Ixelles, Brussels

To play tourist in your own city

Your friends from abroad are coming to see the foodiest city in the world? Show them the Brussels city center in a cool and 100% electric BMW I3, park your DriveNow wherever you want and go to Be My Stoemp. The new hotspot for hot-dogs… with real belgian stoemp inside. Yes, please!

 Where: Be My Stoemp in the center of Brussels

When you need to catch up (and gossip…)

You haven't seen your oldest friends in a long time? Book a table at Ciaooo, a really cool and new italian restaurant in Brussels, to share an original pizza Napoletana just like in Italy. And you won't be late this time because your shared car is waiting right around the corner. Talking all night with good food, that's how we like to spend our nights!

Where: Ciaooo in Schaerbeek, Brussels

Try something new

Do you really need a reason to go out tonight? We thought so too! What about sharing a delicious Lebanese mezze? Awesome food, awesome friends and an awesome restaurant like O Liban. Quickly traveling to another country just for a few hours has never been that easy. Let's try something new tonight!

Where: O Liban in Ixelles, Brussels
